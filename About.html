Summary

           RBD Engineers Pvt. Ltd. is a company manufacturing precision clutch components. They use a sand-casting process for the manufacturing of casting parts. They use sand as the mold material.
     These mold carts are transferred from one place to another. At present trolley carts and rail are used to move mold carts. The layout of the rails placed is such that it acts as a continuous system.
    Therefore, this current system requires lots of workers and time taken for the material handling process is also more which may reduce the productivity of foundry.

          As solution, a mechanism was developed and proposed which works on the principle of Rack and Pinion. In this mechanism, the motor will be mounted on the linear slider (platform)
     having a pinion attached at the motor shaft which will be meshing with the rack of linear guide-way; due to which the platform will be slide on the guide-way. In order to push the mold-
    cart, actuation using lead screw was considered to be placed on the platform such that when the shaft mounted on the leadscrew is extended, it would come in contact at the back
    portion of the mold-cart and will push the mold-cart and when no contact is needed, the shaft would be retracted. This mechanism will be placed parallel to the mold cart rail track and
    require minimal installation set-up which could be beneficial from an industrial point of view.
